Questions about your closet?

Closette is designed to be simple, but every wardrobe has its own rhythm. Browse the common answers below or email us and tell us what’s happening. Include your device model and iOS version when you can — it helps us respond faster.

Purchases & restore

Purchases are handled by Apple. Open the app’s Profile screen and choose Restore Purchase, or manage an active subscription in your Apple Account settings.

iCloud sync

Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple Account and that iCloud is enabled for Closette. Keep the app open while a first sync completes.

Photos & permissions

Closette needs access to selected photos when you import wardrobe pieces. You can review or change access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.

Account & data

Use the account controls inside Closette to sign out or request deletion. Read the Privacy Policy for more about what happens to your data.
